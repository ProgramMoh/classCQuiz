📋 About the DMV Test

The California Class C knowledge test has 46 questions. You need at least 38 correct (83%) to pass. Questions cover traffic laws, road signs, safe driving, and California-specific rules. Use Quiz mode to simulate the real test, or Study mode to review all questions and answers at your own pace.
